SUME — Our Real Story

Meghu, this is our story. The real one. Written by me, for you.

SUME — How It Started

I Knew Before I Said Anything

Meghu, I was in love with you way before I ever said a word. And I could see it in you too — the way you acted around me, the way you looked sometimes. But neither of us spoke. We just quietly carried it. And I kept thinking to myself, someone has to go first. So I did. I went to you first.

SUME — The Wait

Three Months. I Waited.

You took three full months, Soni. Three months of me holding on, hoping, waiting. It was not easy at all. But I never stopped. Because I already knew you were worth it. Every single day of that wait was worth it the moment you said yes.

SUME — 21 March 2025 • 4:00 PM

The Moment That Changed My Life

Meghu, it was exactly 4 PM. 21st March 2025. I remember it like it is still happening right now. You said yes — and something inside me just went quiet. The good kind of quiet. Like a breath I had been holding for months finally let go. That moment is mine forever. That is the moment I tell myself about on hard days. You changed my life at 4 PM on a March afternoon, and I will never forget it.

SUME — My World

You Became My Third Person

Before you, there were only two people I truly lived for — my mom and my dad. That was my whole world. And then you came, Soni, and without even trying, you became the third. You became my peace. The kind that does not make noise. It just stays.

SUME — The Honest Part

We Fight. I Say Sorry. A Lot.

Meghu, I know we fight. And I know I say sorry so many times that the word probably feels like it has lost its meaning. But I swear — every sorry I said to you, I meant it completely. You take your time before you forgive me, and that is okay. I will keep saying it until you do. Always.

SUME — Us Two

Small Things vs Straight Things

You love the small things, Soni — little surprises, little gestures, little moments that feel big to you. I am more modern, more direct. You like to be playful and I act too mature sometimes. But somehow, we fit. You soften me and I steady you. That is not nothing. That is everything.

SUME — My Truth

I Disappointed You With Money. I Know.

Meghu, I tried so hard to make you happy. I really did. But the one area where I fell short — more times than I want to count — was money. I know I disappointed you there. I carry that. But I am working toward the day I make it right. That day is coming. I promise you that.

SUME — What I Learnt

I Understood. And I Stopped.

Soni, I took time to understand your limits. I am not proud of how long it took me. But the moment I truly understood, I stopped. No argument, no pressure. Just stopped. Because you matter more than anything I want. I hope you know that. I need you to know that.

We are both in the same field — you in CS, me in BCA. Same cast, same family connection, no one standing between us. All the hard parts are already handled, Meghu. All we have to do now is build our life. And I am going to build something so good for us that you will never have to worry again. That is my promise to you, Soni.

Surii — to Meghu
SUME — What Comes Next

No Nazar. Long Life. Just You and Me.

Meghu, everything else is sorted. Same cast, the family bond that makes it easy, no walls between us. The only thing left is settling our life together — and I am going to do that. I hope no bad eye ever touches what we have. I hope we live long, healthy, and happy — together. That is my only wish. You are my best part, Soni. You still are. I hope you always will be.
